Must-Visit Destinations

Great Wall of China

Marvel at this ancient wonder stretching over 13,000 miles across northern China. Best sections: Badaling, Mutianyu, and Jinshanling.

Beijing & Northern China

Forbidden City

Explore the world's largest palace complex, home to 24 emperors during Ming and Qing dynasties. Over 9,000 rooms of imperial splendor.

Beijing

Terracotta Army

Witness 8,000 life-sized terracotta soldiers guarding China's first emperor. Each warrior has unique facial features and expressions.

Xi'an

Li River

Cruise through stunning karst landscapes between Guilin and Yangshuo. The scenery appears on the 20 yuan banknote.

Guilin, Guangxi

Temple of Heaven

Visit this masterpiece of Ming architecture where emperors prayed for good harvests. Perfect example of Chinese cosmology in design.

Beijing

Shanghai Skyline

Experience modern China in this global financial hub. Visit the Bund for colonial architecture and Pudong for futuristic skyscrapers.
