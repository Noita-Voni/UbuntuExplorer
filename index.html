Our Story

The Spirit of Ubuntu

Ubuntu Explorer was born from a simple idea: tourism should benefit both travelers and local communities equally. Our name comes from the African philosophy of Ubuntu - "I am because we are" - emphasizing our shared humanity.

Created for the G20 Tourism Hackathon, we're building bridges between cultures through technology. Our AI-powered platform helps travelers discover authentic experiences while ensuring local businesses thrive.
